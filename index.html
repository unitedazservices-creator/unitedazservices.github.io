Our Installation Process

No surprises. No hidden costs. Here's exactly what happens from your first call to the last plank.

1

Free On-Site Estimate

We come to you, measure every room, assess the subfloor, and give you a firm written quote — usually within 24 hours of your call.

2

Material Selection

Choose your LVP from our supplier catalog or supply your own. We help you match style, wear-layer thickness, and budget to your specific rooms.

3

Subfloor Preparation

We remove old flooring if needed, check moisture levels, repair problem areas, and level the surface. This step is what separates a floor that lasts from one that doesn't.

Professional Installation

Clean, precise, and fast. We install with the right method for your subfloor (floating, glue-down, or loose-lay) and leave no seams, gaps, or lifting edges.

We walk every room with you, install all transitions and trim, and take all debris off-site. You're left with a clean home and a floor you're proud of.

Common questions

LVP Flooring FAQ

How long does LVP installation take?
Most single-room or 2–3 room projects are completed in one day. A full home (1,000–1,500 sq ft) typically takes 2–3 days depending on subfloor conditions and layout complexity. We'll give you a specific timeline during your free estimate.
Do I need to buy the materials or do you supply them?
Either works. We price for labor only, so you can purchase LVP yourself from any retailer, or we can source it at contractor pricing — often lower than what you'd find at Home Depot or Lowe's. We'll show you options during the estimate.
Is LVP good for Florida homes?
It's the best option for most Florida homes. LVP is 100% waterproof and humidity-resistant — critical in South Florida's climate. It won't warp, swell, or buckle like hardwood, and it installs well on concrete slab foundations common throughout Broward and Miami-Dade.
What's included in the price?
Our labor price covers: installation of the LVP planks, basic subfloor inspection, standard transitions and trim, and debris removal. Old flooring removal and significant subfloor leveling are priced separately and quoted upfront — never a surprise charge after the job starts.
How do I get a free estimate?
Call or text us at 786-860-4996 or email unitedazservices@gmail.com. We'll schedule a free on-site visit, measure your space, assess your subfloor, and provide a written quote — usually within 24 hours of the visit.
Can you handle large commercial or multi-unit projects?
Yes. We work with property managers and HOAs across South Florida. For multi-unit or commercial projects, we offer volume pricing and priority scheduling. Contact us directly to discuss your scope.
